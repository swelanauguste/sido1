SWING GATE OPENERS

LINEAR SWING GATE OPERATOR - EON SWING GATE ACUATORS

EON is the linear swing gate operator of QUIKO particularly intended for residential applications. Leaves up to 4m can be automated with ease with this Gearmotor, which dimensions allow it to be installed in areas where limited space is available.

The actuators are self-locking and can be unlocked easily in case of electrical blackout by means of the supplied unlocking key. Moreover, the 24V version can be operated even in absence of electricity by means of the optional backup battery unit. Quiko Italy offers inground gate operators in this category that are mostly used while renovating historic buildings. These inground swing gate openers ensure a clean line for the gate to operate. The swing gate openers category also includes other products like articulated arm gear motors that aids in the application of residential swinging gates actuators. These gear motors have the added advantage of easy installation and can be unlocked with ease by means of an apposite key. ROTELLO series swing gate actuators are the irreversible operators that are primarily used in operating swing gates. The linear swing gate operators have a temperate design comprising of key operated manual release system in case of an electricity blackout.

Thanks to the sturdiness of materials used during production, EON is almost maintenance-free and designed to last long.
